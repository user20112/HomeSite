About me

A quick overview of myself and why I made this site.

Hello,

Thanks for taking the time to check out my site, my names Devlin I was a computer programming and engineering major over at the tech in concord. I just finished a project at Osram developing a Machine and line management tool for better automated reporting to SQL, Camstar, and a couple in house applications through an architecture and protocol designed using Lean tools and benchmarking for both the local plant and a few global plants including their production plant in Augsburg Germany.
